SOMO - Hydrodynamic Calculations results pop-up window:

The Show Hydrodynamic Calculations pop-up window reports the most commonly used subset of the hydrodynamic parameters computed by US-SOMO, shown in the picture above. The full list is automatically saved in a file under the extension *.hydro_res. The solvent type and conditions used are also shown in the upper frame.
