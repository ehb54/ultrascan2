AccessGrid Seminar for UltraScan

The AccessGrid Seminar for UltraScan is a forum for UltraScan users to meet virtually on the Internet through AccessGrid video conferencing. The purpose of this monthly seminar is to discuss program features, analyze data, discuss problems and questions and meet with the developer.

All meetings are at the UTHSCSA Bioinformatics Venue Service in the CAUMA room:

Venue Service Address: https://ag1.v19.uthscsa.edu:8000/Venues/default

The time for all meetings will be 11:00 am - 12:00 noon US Central Time. (Westcoast -2, East coast +1, England +6, rest of western Europe +7). There will be 1 hour setup time for testing before the seminar. Please contact Jeremy Mann (210-567-2672, jeremy@biochem.uthscsa.edu) if you have questions/problems with your connection. If needed, additional time can be scheduled after the meeting. More information about the AccessGrid, incl. software, hardware recommendations, and setup instructions can be found at http://www.accessgrid.uthscsa.edu

If you would like me to analyze some of your data during these seminars, please contact me directly a few days before the meeting and send me your data as a zip or tar.gz archive to: demeler@biochem.uthscsa.edu.

Meeting Schedule and Topics:

  • May 5th, 2005:
    Presenter:     Borries Demeler, UTHSCSA, Dept. of Biochemistry .
    Topic: In this seminar I will follow up on a suggestion by Karel Planken, to analyze sedimentation velocity data with various methods and highlight what can be learned from each. We will look at van Holde - Weischet, C(s), second moment, finite element fitting, and dc/dt. If you would like to submit some data for this seminar, please do so in advance by e-mailing me a zip or tar.gz archive of the directory containing your data. 1 dataset per participant, please.

This is a promising new method designed to obtain high-resolution molecular weight distributions from sedimentation velocity experiments. I will expand on the talk given at the 14th intl. AUC conference in Lausanne, Switzerland.

    Presenter:     Borries Demeler, UTHSCSA, Dept. of Biochemistry.
    Topic: In this seminar I will demonstrate the new C(s) implementation in UltraScan. I will show the program and discuss the options. The demonstration will include analysis of real and simulated data. Analysis results are compared to van Holde - Weischet analyses.

  • January 6th, 2005:
    Presenter:     Borries Demeler, UTHSCSA, Dept. of Biochemistry.
    Topic: This meeting will be a free for all. You can upload your data to the AccessGrid and I will edit & analyze it while you watch. I will answer any questions and discuss ways to improve your data (if applicable, of course) and discuss experimental design issues. You should have your data available in tar.gz or zip archive form (a single file for all raw data). If you already edited your data using UltraScan then you can use the archive feature of UltraScan to create an UltraScan archive.

  • December 2nd, 2004:
    Presenter:     Borries Demeler, UTHSCSA, Dept. of Biochemistry.
    Topic: An overview of the new UltraScan LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System), the UltraScan hydrodynamic database and UltraScan and web interfaces for this system. This system is designed to aid in the management of data, result presentation, and coordination of multi-user/investigator facilities. I will discuss how the investigators can provide information over a web interface, and review their data and analysis results from UltraScan over the web, and interface with a relational database from both UltraScan and from the web interface.
